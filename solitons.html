Literary Review of a Solitons Papers

Summary

Wrote a literary review and prepared a presentation for the class on Yoritaka Iwatas 2004 paper 'Solitons in nuclear time-dependent density functional theory'. The paper dealt with the application of soliton equations (namely 2-soliton KdV equation) to many-nucleon bodies. This was done to achieve a predictable and suitable model for these systems.
